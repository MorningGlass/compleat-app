Compleat

A simple, modern iOS app to track your Munro-bagging progress

Download on the App Store

Track Your Progress

Record every ascent with dates and personal notes. Watch your completion percentage grow and see detailed statistics including total elevation climbed across all 282 Munros.

Interactive Map

Browse all Munros on a map with colour-coded pins. Filter to see only climbed or remaining summits.

Regional Breakdown

Explore Scotland's mountain regions with dedicated views for each area. Track your progress region by region with visual progress indicators.

Smart List View

Search, sort, and filter all 282 Munros by name, height, region, or number of ascents. Bulk-add multiple peaks for multi-summit days on the hills.
