Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2026

Overview

Compleat ("the App") is designed with your privacy in mind. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection

The App stores your Munro completion data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

Data Storage

All data you enter into the App, including your progress and preferences, is stored locally on your device. If you use iCloud sync, your data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is subject to Apple's privacy policy.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the App Store.
